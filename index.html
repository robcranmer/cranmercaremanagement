CQC

CQC readiness aligned to the 5 key questions

We support providers to prepare for inspection using a clear, evidence-led structure based on the CQC’s five key questions. Below are practical examples of what we focus on and how we support you to evidence it.

Safe

How you prevent harm and manage risk

We support you to show strong risk management, safer staffing, safeguarding practice, and consistent learning from incidents.

  • Safeguarding: clear processes, training, escalation and robust records
  • Medicines: audits, competency, MAR accuracy and oversight
  • Risk: risk assessments reviewed, personalised and actioned
  • Incidents: learning shared and improvements tracked
Open adult safeguarding resources Ask for a Safe audit
Effective

How you achieve good outcomes

We support you to evidence effective care planning, staff competence, outcome measurement, and quality improvement cycles.

  • Care planning: needs-led, personalised and reviewed
  • Training: matrix, competency checks, supervision quality
  • Audits: clear cycles with actions and re-audits
  • Outcomes: evidence of progress and quality impact
Quality & governance links Request an evidence review
Caring

How you deliver person-centred, compassionate care

We support you to evidence dignity, respect, involvement, and a culture where people feel safe and heard.

  • Person-centred care: lived experience reflected in plans
  • Feedback: collected, acted on, and shared with learning
  • Culture: values-led practice across teams
  • Communication: accessible info and involvement
Improve feedback systems What “Outstanding” looks like
Responsive

How you adapt to people’s needs

We support you to show flexible care delivery, strong complaints handling, and good outcomes when needs change.

  • Access: timely support and clear pathways
  • Changes in needs: reviews completed and acted on
  • Complaints: handled well with learning and improvement
  • Capacity & consent: MCA aligned practice where relevant
Review complaints handling MCA resources
Well-led

How leadership and governance drive quality

We support you to build governance that’s simple, consistent, and evidence-led — demonstrating oversight and continuous improvement.

  • Governance rhythm: monthly pack, KPIs, actions tracked
  • Audits and learning: visible improvements and accountability
  • Leadership: clear roles, supervision quality, culture
  • Openness: learning culture and transparent communication
