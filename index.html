Care management, governance & quality improvement

Cranmer Care Management Ltd supports care providers and organisations to deliver safe, effective and person-centred care. We specialise in clinical governance, supported living, and mental health services — with practical, evidence-led support that strengthens compliance, improves outcomes and prepares services for inspection.

Supported living Mental health Clinical governance Inspection readiness

Based in Yorkshire, delivering improvement support across the region and beyond.

CQC-focused improvement with a proven Yorkshire track record

We bring hands-on experience supporting services to achieve and sustain strong inspection outcomes. Our approach is rooted in robust clinical governance, clear leadership, measurable action planning, and practical support that improves quality on the ground.

Where we’ve supported quality and compliance

Our experience includes work alongside organisations and community partners across Bradford and Yorkshire, including supported living and mental health settings. We understand the evidence CQC expects and how to translate standards into day-to-day practice.

  • Vitality Living Ltd (supported living)
  • Bradford District Care NHS Foundation Trust (mental health / community services)
  • Bradford City Football Club (mental health support & community collaboration)

Clinical governance that stands up to scrutiny

We help you build a governance framework that is simple, consistent and inspection-ready: audits, learning from incidents, risk management, policy implementation, supervision and training, KPI dashboards, and evidence packs aligned to CQC’s key lines of enquiry.

  • Supported living compliance & provider oversight
  • Mental health service quality & safety systems
  • Action plans that are measurable, owned and delivered

Services available

Practical, hands-on support across supported living, mental health and care delivery — from quick improvements to full turnaround programmes.

Mock inspections (CQC-style)

Structured mock inspections with clear findings, evidence requirements and an improvement plan aligned to CQC expectations.

Turnaround management

Rapid stabilisation and sustained improvement for challenged services: risk review, governance reset, action planning, staff support and performance recovery.

Action planning & delivery

SMART action plans with ownership, timelines, KPIs and follow-up — ensuring improvements happen, not just get written down.

Supported living support

Provider oversight, quality audits, person-centred planning, risk management and outcomes-based support for supported living services.

Mental health quality improvement

Governance, incident learning, audit programmes, supervision structures and evidence packs to strengthen safe and effective mental health support.

Recruitment & human resources support

HR support to strengthen recruitment pipelines, safer recruitment practice, onboarding, role profiles, supervision frameworks and workforce retention.
