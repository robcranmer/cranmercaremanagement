CQC

Inspection readiness built around CQC key questions and KLOEs

Our mock inspections, evidence support and action planning are aligned to the CQC approach, the five key questions (Safe, Effective, Caring, Responsive and Well-led) and the Key Lines of Enquiry (KLOEs).

Safe

We test safeguarding, risk management, staffing and medicines using CQC-style prompts.

  • S1: Safeguard people from abuse (systems, processes and practice).
  • S2: Assess and manage risks while respecting people‚Äôs freedom.
  • S3: Sufficient numbers of suitable staff.
  • S4: Medicines managed safely (where applicable).

Effective

We focus on evidence-based care, staff competence and outcomes that show impact.

  • E1: Needs/choices assessed and care delivered in line with legislation and evidence-based guidance.
  • E2: Staff have the skills, knowledge and experience to deliver effective care.
  • Training, supervision, competency and learning culture.

Caring & Responsive

We check real lived experience, person-centred care and how services respond to needs and feedback.

  • C1: Kindness, respect, compassion and emotional support.
  • R1: Personalised care responsive to people‚Äôs needs.
  • R2: Concerns/complaints listened to, responded to and used to improve quality.
