Tender writing

Bid & tender writing that strengthens your case and improves win rates

We assist you to produce clear, compliant, evidence-led submissions for local authority, ICB/NHS and framework tenders — written in the right tone, with strong methodology, staffing, governance and quality assurances.

What makes bids score well

  • Mapped answers: aligned to the question and evaluation criteria
  • Golden Thread: consistent story from outcomes → method → staffing → governance
  • Credibility: evidence libraries, policies, audits, training and governance referenced properly
  • Efficiency: templates and reusable content to save time

CQC

CQC readiness built around the 5 key lines of enquiry

We use a clear, evidence-led approach aligned to the CQC’s five key questions. You’ll know what to evidence, what to improve, and how to demonstrate learning and assurance.

Safe

Risk, safeguarding, medicines, incident learning, staffing and prevention.

  • Risk assessments and reviews
  • Safeguarding practice and records
  • Medicines management assurance
Effective

Outcomes, training, competency, care planning, audits and improvement cycles.

  • Training matrix and competency
  • Audit cycles that show learning
  • Care plan quality and outcomes
Caring

Person-centred care, dignity, respect, involvement and compassion.

  • Feedback & co-production
  • Culture and practice observation
  • Compliments and learning shared
Responsive

Access, personalised support, complaints handling and adapting to needs.

  • Needs-led care delivery
  • Complaints management assurance
  • Capacity, consent and best interest
Well-led

Leadership, governance, oversight, openness, learning culture and sustainability.

  • Provider governance pack
  • Quality meeting rhythm
  • Action tracking and accountability
