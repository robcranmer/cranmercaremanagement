Tender writing

Bid & tender writing that improves clarity and win rates

We assist you to produce clear, compliant, evidence-led submissions for local authority, ICB/NHS and framework tenders — written in the right tone, with strong methodology, staffing, governance and quality assurance.

Discuss a bid

What makes bids score well

  • Mapped answers: aligned to the question and evaluation criteria
  • Golden Thread: consistent story from outcomes → method → staffing → governance
  • Credibility: evidence libraries, policies, audits and training referenced properly
  • Efficiency: templates + reusable content to save time

How we can support

  • Review & rewrite for scoring improvement
  • Full bid build (from scratch)
  • Method statements, staffing models, governance
  • Evidence library and compliant appendices

Pricing: fixed-fee packages or day rate options — always transparent.

CQC

CQC readiness aligned to the 5 key lines of enquiry

We use a clear, evidence-led approach aligned to the CQC’s five key questions. You’ll know what to evidence, what to improve, and how to demonstrate learning and assurance.

Safe

Risk, safeguarding, medicines, incident learning, staffing and prevention.

Effective

Outcomes, training, competency, care planning, audits and improvement cycles.

Caring

Person-centred care, dignity, respect, involvement and compassion.

Responsive

Access, personalised support, complaints handling and adapting to needs.

Well-led

Leadership, governance, oversight, openness, learning culture and sustainability.
